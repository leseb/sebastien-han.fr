Inktank's guys are awesome!

<!--more-->

Thanks to that article I would like to highlight the professionalism of the Inktank's  guys. That week I had a bad time with Ceph after a new CRUSH map injection. I went directly to the Ceph IRC channel to find some help. I quickly got an answer from 'sjust'. He quickly offered me his help, I explained my situation and neither one nor two, told me to upload my files (logs, binary) to a special sftp server dedicated to this purpose.

Regardless of the fact that it was due to a mistake/type of mine, he had made a great effort to help me. Of course I mentionned "sjust" but all the guys on IRC are always pleased to help.

>It's one thing to talk the talk about open source, and another to walk the walk.

That's just one simple example to show you what kind of attitude these guys have...personally I find it just awesome. So **thanks** again! Needliess to say that I'll never hesitate to subscribe to a premium support from Inktank.
